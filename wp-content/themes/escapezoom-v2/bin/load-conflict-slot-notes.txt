Load / conflict on one slot
===========================

Goal: two customers complete checkout for the same product_id + sans_time; second should hit conflict branch or validation block.

Steps:
1. Pick a near-future slot with capacity 1.
2. User A: complete payment → booking row status=1.
3. User B: attempt checkout for same slot — expect validation error from ez_booking_conflict_* or pipeline updates status to conflict with refund_conflict_once.

Watch: [ez_order_flow] pipeline_exit and booking_ok; no double close on wp_zb_booking_history for same slot (unique constraint / business rules).

WP-CLI: wp ez-report-pending-sans — lists pending orders missing sans_time (report-only).
